About

Five Island Asset Management is a New York-based investment manager. The firm specializes in below investment grade credit products, including corporate bonds, leveraged loans and structured products. Five Island seeks to generate attractive risk-adjusted returns while stressing the preservation of capital. Five Island seeks to achieve this objective through the application of extensive fundamental analysis and value investing principles. Five Island is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Harbinger Group, Inc.
